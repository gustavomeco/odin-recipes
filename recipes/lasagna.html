Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is an Italian dish consisting of layered wide, flat pasta sheets with fillings such as meat sauce (ragù), béchamel sauce, cheese (like mozzarella, ricotta, and Parmesan), and sometimes vegetables, which are baked in an oven until hot and melty.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Salt + black pepper

Steps

  1. Cook the noodles
    • If using regular noodles, boil until just al dente.
    • If using no-boil noodles, skip this step.
  2. Make the meat sauce
    • Heat oil in a pan.
    • Add ground beef, season with salt and pepper, and cook until browned.
    • Pour in tomato sauce and let it simmer 10–15 minutes.
  3. Layer the lasagna (in a baking dish)
    • Spread a thin layer of sauce on the bottom.
    • Add a layer of noodles.
    • Add meat sauce on top.
    • Sprinkle mozzarella (and parmesan if you have).
    • Repeat layers until you run out, finishing with sauce + cheese on top.
  4. Bake
    • Cover with foil and bake at 180°C (350°F)for about 30 minutes.
    • Remove foil and bake another 10–15 minutes until cheese is melted and golden
  5. Rest & serve
    • Let it sit for 10 minutes before cutting, so it holds together.
